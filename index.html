FAQ

Do I need to make an appointment in advance?
It's recommended to book your appointment in advance to ensure your spot and prompt service, especially on weekends and holidays.
Are the products you use safe?
All of our products are carefully selected, skin-friendly, have clear origins, and are completely safe to use.
How long does a typical nail or lash service take?
Basic nail services usually take about 45–60 minutes, while lash extensions take about 60–90 minutes depending on the specific service and your requirements.
Is there anything I should keep in mind after finishing my service?
Avoid exposing your new nails or lashes to hot water and strong impacts. Our staff will give you detailed advice after your service is completed.
